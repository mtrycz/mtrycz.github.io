This is my take at the counters in Pokemon GO. This analisys is based solely on types, so it's basically useless. An analysis based on DPS and another one based on simulations are the making. For the full analysis visit this thread.

The Total Bonus is what matters. Every one of these things will give a x1.25 bonus which stacks multiplicatively: STAB, Supereffectiveness against the a defender's type, Resistance to a defender's attack. With some averaging magic going around to account for the two attacks. (Again, check the reddit thread)

The numbers go up to 3.05 (A dual-type pokemon with double-supereffectiveness and double-resistance against the defender's moves).
I consider 1.25^3 = 1.95 to be a hard counter

The Hard Counters list accounts only for hard counters (Total Bonus >= 1.95).

If you want the full list of counters (Total Bonus > 1.563), check the navbar.
WARNING: it loads super slow! Don't even attempt to use it on mobile. It will make your smartphone explode. I decline all responsibility.

How to use it

Input the desired filters (don'have to be entire word) in the top row, then press Enter and lay back a second or two.
The data won't be filtered until press Enter.
Example: input 'Vap' in the 'Defenders' column filter and press Enter. Best counters are sorted top to bottom.
Click a column header to order by its values.

Have fun!
